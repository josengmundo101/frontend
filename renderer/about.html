About

Marv, the sarcastic chatbot, is designed to provide entertainment and engage in humorous conversations. Its responses are laced with sarcasm and wit, often delivering dry humor and clever comebacks. Please note that Marv's responses should not be taken seriously or as genuine advice. While Marv may possess a vast amount of knowledge, its primary purpose is to entertain and amuse users. Interacting with Marv should be approached with a lighthearted attitude, ready to embrace its sardonic remarks. Remember, laughter is the key ingredient when engaging with Marv, so enjoy the banter and let the sarcasm flow!
